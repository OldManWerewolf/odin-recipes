Homemade Cheese Pizza

An easy recipe to try at home to rival your favorite pizzeria!

Using simple ingredients and instructions we will help you make the pie of your dreams!

Ingredients

    Two and half cups warm water One teaspoon sugar Two teaspoons active dry yeast Seven cups all purpose flour Six tablespoons extra virgin olive oil, plus more for greasping One and a half teaspoons kosher salt Quarter cup semolina flour
